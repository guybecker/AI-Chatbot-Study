What To Consider In A Portable Blender

Battery Life

Look for models that can handle multiple blending sessions on a single charge. The best portable blenders offer 15-20 uses before needing a recharge.

Blending Power

While portable blenders can't match full-size models, the best ones can handle frozen fruits and leafy greens without leaving chunks. Look for models with at least 200 watts of power.

Ease of Cleaning

Portable blenders should be easy to clean, ideally with removable blades and dishwasher-safe components. The flip-top design of the NutriBullet eliminates the need for separate cups.
