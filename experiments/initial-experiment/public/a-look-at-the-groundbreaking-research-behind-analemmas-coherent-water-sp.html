A Look at the Groundbreaking Research Behind Aǹalemma’s Coherent Water

A group of researchers in the Netherlands explored the role of water and light in living systems. They realized that water plays an extraordinary role in the regulation of biological systems. This realization led them down a far different path than one may have thought. They found out, water currently exists in a deeply chaotic state. However, their real discovery was that water could in fact be brought into a highly ordered or “coherent” state by mimicking nature’s rhythms and cycles. This is the beginning of the story of Aǹalemma, a company born from nearly two decades of scientific inquiry into how structured water interacts with living organisms. Its solution is now encapsulated in a quartz wand that can turn regular water into its full-spectrum coherent state.

Deeply committed to empirical validation, Aǹalemma worked with third-party, independent institutions to conduct a series of rigorously controlled studies that aimed to measure the physiological impacts of coherent water. Among these, two studies stand out for their implications on human health and performance.

The first study, conducted in 2022, focused on adenosine triphosphate (ATP). ATP is often described as the molecular fuel that powers nearly every biological function in the human body, from muscle contractions and cell signaling to brain activity and DNA replication. Acknowledging ATP’s role, Aǹalemma initiated a double-blind, placebo-controlled clinical trial to investigate whether its coherent water could influence ATP levels in humans.

Thirty-eight healthy adults between the ages of 18 and 60 were randomly assigned to two groups. Over a 60-day period, one group consumed at least 1.5 liters of Aǹalemma-treated water daily. Meanwhile, the other drank untreated water.

The results were striking. Participants who drank Aǹalemma water saw a statistically significant 19.52% increase in ATP levels. This is more than twice the improvement seen in the placebo group. This ATP boost has wide-reaching benefits on almost every system within the human body. For instance, given that the brain consumes approximately a quarter of the body’s ATP, even modest increases can support enhanced cognitive function.

It’s worth noting that Aǹalemma also explored neurological impacts, specifically how coherent water might influence brainwave activity. In a preliminary 2019 case study involving identical twins, researchers used cutting-edge technology to measure changes in brain coherence after water consumption. One twin drank Aǹalemma water while the other consumed regular water.

The twin who drank the coherent water showed immediate and sustained improvements in delta brainwave coherence. This frequency is linked to deep relaxation, emotional stability, and internal processing. The data, while early state, hinted that Aǹalemma water can normalize and stabilize brainwave activity, potentially improving how different regions of the brain communicate in real-time.

These studies illustrate that when structured according to natural rhythms, water can serve as a subtle but powerful modulator of biological function. Aǹalemma’s research opens a provocative new chapter in understanding hydration and health. Larger and longer-term studies will be necessary to confirm these effects at scale. Still, the early evidence is promising and suggests that how people interact with water may soon change dramatically.
